ABOUT NAYEPANKH

Creating Opportunities, Inspiring Change, Transforming Futures.

At Nayepankh Foundation, we believe every child deserves access to education, opportunity and a future filled with hope. Through dedicated community initiatives and sustainable development programs, we empower individuals to rise above challenges and achieve their full potential.

Nayepankh foundation was initiated to bring a change and help people during the tough times of Covid. Later in the year, the NGO started to expand their operations and provide help to a wider section of the society
